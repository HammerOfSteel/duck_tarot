# Peace, Love, and Poultry: The Moomin-Style Tarot

A whimsical tarot deck combining the aesthetic charm of Tove Jansson's Moomin illustrations with the wisdom of historical figures and the delightful presence of ducks.

## Art Style

Early Tove Jansson/Moominvalley aesthetic featuring:
- Fine, expressive black ink-line drawings with delicate cross-hatching
- Soft watercolor washes in muted, earthy tones (sage, dusty blue, warm grey)
- A balance of cozy domesticity and the vast, mysterious Nordic wilderness
- Figures with soft, expressive features

## Contents

- **[overview.md](overview.md)** - Complete descriptions of all 78 cards in the deck
  - 22 Major Arcana cards featuring enlightened thinkers and visionaries
  - 56 Minor Arcana cards across four suits:
    - Cups (Emotions, Home, & Healing)
    - Pentacles (Work, Nature, & Material Joy)
    - Swords (Intellect, Truth, & The Mind)
    - Wands (Energy, Passion, & Movement)

## Featured Figures

The Major Arcana features historical luminaries including:
- Alan Watts, Nikola Tesla, Hilma af Klint
- The Dalai Lama, Rumi, Malala Yousafzai
- Friedrich Nietzsche, Galileo Galilei, Nelson Mandela
- Carl Sagan, William Shakespeare, ABBA
- And many more...

Each thoughtfully paired with duck companions in meaningful scenes.
