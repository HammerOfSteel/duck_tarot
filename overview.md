# Peace, Love, and Poultry: The Moomin-Style Tarot

## Art Style Guide

Early Tove Jansson/Moominvalley aesthetic. Fine, expressive black ink-line drawings with delicate cross-hatching. Soft watercolor washes in muted, earthy tones (sage, dusty blue, warm grey). A balance of cozy domesticity and the vast, mysterious Nordic wilderness. Figures should have soft, expressive features.

## 🃏 The Major Arcana: The Enlightened Quackers

### 0. The Fool (Alan Watts)
A man with a joyful, twinkling-eyed beard and a rucksack skipping toward a misty cliff. He is laughing at the sky. A small duckling is tugging at his trouser leg in warning.

![The Fool - Alan Watts](the_fool_alan_watts.png)

### I. The Magician (Nikola Tesla)
Tesla standing at a weathered wooden table with a vintage copper coil. Sparks of electricity above his hand take the shape of a glowing duck. A real duck sits on the table wearing a tiny wire monocle.

![The Magician - Nikola Tesla](the_magician_nikola_tesla.png)

### II. The High Priestess (Hilma af Klint)
Sitting in a mystical, quiet forest. She is painting a giant, abstract pastel circle on a canvas. Nearby, ducks swim in a pond, their ripples forming geometric spirals that match her art.

![The High Priestess - Hilma af Klint](the_high_priestess_hilma_af_klint.png)

### III. The Empress (Catherine the Great)
Sitting in a lush, overgrown garden on a velvet chair. She wears a heavy robe with a subtle duck-feather pattern. She holds a golden bowl overflowing with grain for a happy flock at her feet.

![The Empress - Catherine the Great](the_empress_catherine_the_great.png)

### IV. The Emperor (Carl Linnaeus)
Sitting on a mossy stone in a Swedish pine forest, writing in a large ledger. He is observing a group of ducks. Beside him, a large, regal Mallard stands like a royal guard.

![The Emperor - Carl Linnaeus](the_emperor_carl_linnaeus.png)

### V. The Hierophant (The Dalai Lama)
Sitting cross-legged under a Bodhi tree with a gentle smile. A duck is perched on each of his shoulders; they all look like they are sharing a silent, profound joke.

![The Hierophant - The Dalai Lama](the_hierophant_dalai_lama.png)

### VI. The Lovers (Rumi)
A poet standing in a swirling wind of rose petals. In the foreground, two ducks are tucked together under a willow tree, their necks leaning in to form a soft heart shape.

![The Lovers - Rumi](the_lovers_rumi.png)

### VII. The Chariot (Amelia Earhart)
Sitting in an open-cockpit plane made of weathered wood. The nose of the plane is shaped like a giant duck bill. She looks determined, flying through soft, stylized Moomin clouds.

![The Chariot - Amelia Earhart](the_chariot_amelia_earhart.png)

### VIII. Strength (Malala Yousafzai)
Standing in a field of wildflowers and tall grass. She is gently petting the head of a very large, wild, and temperamental-looking goose, taming it with a peaceful gaze.

![Strength - Malala Yousafzai](strength_malala_yousafzai.png)

### IX. The Hermit (Friedrich Nietzsche)
A man with a massive, bushy mustache sitting in a dark, cozy cave by a small lantern. He is staring intensely into the eyes of a duck. The duck is staring back, looking equally philosophical.

![The Hermit - Friedrich Nietzsche](the_hermit_friedrich_nietzsche.png)

### X. Wheel of Fortune (Galileo Galilei)
Looking through an old wooden telescope. The sky behind him is a giant spinning wheel of stars, but the planets are actually golden duck eggs orbiting a sun.

![Wheel of Fortune - Galileo Galilei](wheel_of_fortune_galileo_galilei.png)

### XI. Justice (Nelson Mandela)
Standing before a simple wooden table. On it is a scale: one side has a heavy iron weight, the other has a single white duck feather. The feather is lower, outweighing the iron.

![Justice - Nelson Mandela](justice_nelson_mandela.png)

### XII. The Hanged Man (Emanuel Swedenborg)
Hanging upside down by one foot from a magical Rowan tree. He looks perfectly serene, watching a duck swim "upside down" in the reflection of a still pond below him.

![The Hanged Man - Emanuel Swedenborg](the_hanged_man_emanuel_swedenborg.png)

### XIII. Death (The Buddha)
Sitting in meditation under a tree. In front of him, a duck is shedding its old, grey feathers, and shimmering, golden new ones are growing underneath.

![Death - The Buddha](death_buddah.png)

### XIV. Temperance (Mahatma Gandhi)
Sitting by a slow-moving river, pouring water from one ceramic jug to another. A duck is floating calmly in the stream exactly where the water lands.

### XV. The Devil (Edgar Allan Poe)
Sitting in a dark, gothic library with ink stains everywhere. He is mesmerized by a "Raven-Duck" (a duck with pitch-black feathers) perched on a bust of Pallas. The duck is holding a piece of bread it refuses to share.

![The Devil - Edgar Allan Poe](the_devil_edgar_alan_poe.png)

### XVI. The Tower (The Vasa Ship)
The famous Swedish ship tilting in the water. Instead of disaster, the scene is whimsical: ducks are diving off the ornate wooden deck like it's a giant toy, salvaging bread from the water.

![The Tower - The Vasa Ship](the_tower_vasa.png)

### XVII. The Star (Carl Sagan)
Standing on a dark hill at night, pointing at a constellation shaped like a duck. "We are made of duck-stuff." The water below reflects the starlight in soft indigo washes.

![The Star - Carl Sagan](the_start_carl_sagan.png)

### XVIII. The Moon (William Shakespeare)
Standing on a dark stage lit by a giant moon with a duck’s face. He holds a white porcelain duck skull in his hand, looking at it pensively.
![The Moon - William Shakespeare](the_moon_william_shakespeare.png)
### XIX. The Sun (ABBA)
Four figures in glittery, 70s-style jumpsuits and tiny platform boots, dancing on a wooden Swedish jetty. A giant, smiling sun shines behind them, and four ducks in the water are mimicking their dance moves.

![The Sun - ABBA](the_sun_ABBA.png)

### XX. Judgement (Ludwig van Beethoven)
Standing on a craggy mountain top, conducting a storm. Instead of thunder, the "sound" is a massive, harmonious "QUACK" visualized in the clouds, waking the valley below.

![Judgement - Ludwig van Beethoven](judgement_ludwig_van_beethoven.png)

### XXI. The World (David Attenborough)
Standing in the center of a lush, green globe. He is surrounded by every species of duck—Mallards, Wood ducks, Teals—all living in a perfect, peaceful circle.

![The World - David Attenborough](the_world_david_attenborough.png)

## 🌊 The Lesser Arcana: The Four Ducky Realms

### The Suit of Cups (Emotions, Home, & Healing)

**Ace:** A child's small hand holding a ceramic cup overflowing with bubbles. A tiny duckling pops its head out of the foam.

**Two:** A young couple sitting on a pier, sharing a drink. Their shadows on the wood are shaped like two ducks touching beaks.

**Three:** Three friends in a cozy kitchen laughing over tea. Their sweaters have different duck patterns.

**Four:** A contemplative boy sitting under a tree, ignoring three cups of water on the grass. A duck sits nearby, looking at a fourth cup floating in the air.

**Five:** A figure in a dark cloak standing by a grey river, mourning three spilled cups. Two ducks are swimming away with the remaining two cups.

**Six:** A warm, nostalgic scene. A girl child in a Victorian clawfoot tub, surrounded by six yellow rubber ducks. Soft light through a window.

**Seven:** A dreamer standing before seven glowing clouds. Inside the clouds are duck-themed treasures: a golden egg, a feather crown, a giant loaf of bread.

**Eight:** A figure in a traveling cloak (like Snufkin) walking away from eight cups toward a dark mountain. A single duck follows at a distance.

**Nine:** A cheerful person sitting on a wooden bench, looking satisfied. Behind them on a shelf are nine cups, each with a duck painted on it.

**Ten:** A family standing on a hill under a rainbow. They are looking down at a pond where ten ducks are swimming in a perfect line.

**Page:** A youthful figure in a blue tunic standing by the sea, looking in surprise at a cup that has a small, talking duck inside it.

**Knight:** A romantic figure riding a slow, white horse across a stream. He carries a cup, and his cape has a large mallard embroidered on it.

**Queen:** A serene woman sitting on a throne of driftwood by the shore, looking into a cup. A white duck rests calmly at her feet.

**King:** A kind-looking man sitting in a wide chair by a fireplace, holding a cup. A large, regal duck sits on the arm of his chair.

### The Suit of Pentacles (Work, Nature, & Material Joy)

**Ace:** A gardener's hands cupping a single, giant golden egg found in a nest of soft moss and feathers.

**Two:** A street performer balancing on one leg, juggling two coins. A duck nearby is also balancing on one leg, watching intently.

**Three:** A master carpenter and two apprentices in a workshop, carving a giant wooden duck. Three coins are embedded in the workbench.

**Four:** A grumpy man sitting on a trunk, clutching four golden eggs. He wears a hat with a duck feather, looking wary of a real duck approaching him.

**Five:** Two figures in tattered clothes walking through a snowstorm past a glowing church window. A small, cold duck huddles in the arms of one of them.

**Six:** A wealthy traveler on a bench, handing out bread and coins to a group of townspeople. Ducks congregate around to catch the crumbs.

**Seven:** A farmer leaning on a hoe, looking at a bush where seven golden eggs have grown. A duck is pecking at the dirt nearby.

**Eight:** A craftsperson sitting in a cozy cottage, painting small wooden ducks. Eight finished ones sit on a shelf.

**Nine:** A woman in a beautiful gown standing in a lush garden of sunflowers. A pet duck with a silk ribbon around its neck sits on her wrist.

**Ten:** An elderly couple sitting outside their house, watching their grandchildren play. Ten golden eggs are hidden around the garden like an Easter hunt.

**Page:** A studious youth in a garden, holding a golden coin and comparing its weight to a duck egg.

**Knight:** A sturdy worker on a heavy farm horse, riding through a field of grain. A duck is perched on the horse’s mane.

**Queen:** A motherly figure sitting in a garden of herbs and vegetables, a golden egg in her lap and a duck resting in her shadow.

**King:** A successful merchant in a cozy office filled with books. He holds a coin, and a large mallard sits on his desk like a paperweight.

### The Suit of Swords (Intellect, Truth, & The Mind)

**Ace:** A hand reaching from a cloud, holding a sharp, white quill-pen (a duck feather). The air around it ripples like water.

**Two:** A girl sitting on the shore, blindfolded, holding two long reeds crossed like swords. A duck sits on each side of her, perfectly still.

**Three:** A heart-shaped lily pad floating on a dark, rainy pond, pierced by three sharp feathers. No figures, just a moody, lonely atmosphere.

**Four:** A figure resting on a stone sarcophagus in a quiet chapel. Four swords are carved into the stone, but a single yellow rubber duck sits on the figure’s chest.

**Five:** A figure standing on a beach, collecting five swords/reeds. Other people walk away in the distance. A duck watches from the tall grass.

**Six:** A boatman rowing a figure and a child across a grey lake. Six swords stand in the boat. A duck swims alongside the boat, guiding the way.

**Seven:** A sneaky character creeping away from a camp, carrying five swords. They are wearing a duck-shaped mask to hide their face.

**Eight:** The Specific Scene: A girl stands in a shallow marsh, her eyes bandaged and her body lightly bound with long duck-grass. Eight sharp feathers are stuck in the mud around her. Several ducks watch her silently from the reeds.

**Nine:** A person sitting up in bed at night, head in hands. Nine swords hang on the wall like shadows. A duck sits on the foot of the bed, looking equally troubled.

**Ten:** A figure lying face-down on a dark beach, ten feathers stuck in their back. A duck is gently preening the figure's hair with its bill in a gesture of comfort.

**Page:** A young person standing on a windy hill, holding a sword and looking at the horizon. Their scarf is blowing in the wind and has a duck pattern.

**Knight:** A figure on a charging horse, sword unsheathed, riding through a storm. A flock of ducks is flying in the same direction, matching the speed.

**Queen:** A sharp-eyed woman sitting on a stone throne in the clouds. She holds a sword. Her crown is made of silver duck feathers.

**King:** A wise man sitting in a high chair, a sword in his hand. He looks stern but fair. A duck is carved into the top of his wooden throne.

### The Suit of Wands (Energy, Passion, & Movement)

**Ace:** A hand holding a staff that is sprouting fresh green leaves. At the tip, a small carved duck head glows with a warm light.

**Two:** A figure standing on a balcony, holding a staff and looking at a globe. A duck sits on the railing, looking at the same horizon.

**Three:** A merchant on a cliffside, watching three ships in the bay. Each ship has a duck-shaped figurehead.

**Four:** Two figures dancing between four tall staves decorated with flowers. A group of ducks is "dancing" in a circle around their feet.

**Five:** Five youths in a clearing, playfully fighting with sticks. They are all wearing hats with different colored duck feathers.

**Six:** A figure riding a horse through a cheering crowd, holding a staff with a laurel wreath. A duck sits on the rider's shoulder, also wearing a tiny wreath.

**Seven:** A person on a hill defending themselves with a staff against six others. They have a determined expression; a duck at their feet is hissing at the "enemies."

**Eight:** Eight staves (reeds) flying through a clear blue sky in perfect formation, like a flock of migrating ducks.

**Nine:** A weary traveler leaning on a staff, a bandage on their head. Eight other staves form a fence behind them. A duck sits by their feet, keeping watch.

**Ten:** A figure bowed low, carrying a heavy bundle of ten staves. A small duck is sitting on top of the heavy bundle, "helping" by hitching a ride.

**Page:** An energetic youth standing in the desert, reading a scroll. Their tunic is bright yellow with orange duck-foot prints.

**Knight:** A bold figure on a galloping horse, holding a flaming staff. The horse’s blanket is decorated with flying mallards.

**Queen:** A warm woman sitting on a throne decorated with sunflowers. She holds a staff. A black duck sits at her feet, and she holds a sunflower in her other hand.

**King:** A powerful man on a throne decorated with lions and ducks. He holds a flowering staff. He looks full of "Peace and Love" energy and creative fire.
